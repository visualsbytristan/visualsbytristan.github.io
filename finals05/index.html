Transform from within

Are you ready for
a change?

Leap Into Style offers a holistic approach to transformation that combines personal styling and life coaching into one service. We believe in taking you on an inside-out journey of self-discovery to uncover your authentic style and live your best life! A life that unleashes your true identity and unlocks your beauty from within. If you are at a point where you want to know who you really are then this service is for you. This service is for people who feel stuck in life and don't know how or where to start. You know you feel stuck when you can identify with the following:

  • Poor self-image
  • Negative self-perception
  • Suffering from depression
  • Insecure
  • Low confidence
  • Lack of self-worth

It's time to break through! Allow us to shine the light on your style store - one that speaks your heart and your truth! Our mission is to empower each and every person to embrace their identity in Christ! Come, take the leap!

Meet Nicole

Over the past 5 years, I have sat down with men & women who are eager to take a leap into getting to know their identity and style.

Fashion for me is all about exploring and having fun with style. I don't believe in following any rules or trends. The way to know your authentic is digging deeper into your style story and getting to the core of your heart.

At Leap Into Style, I walk alongside people to help rediscover their identity through Christ. There is something powerful when the transfomation is from God.
