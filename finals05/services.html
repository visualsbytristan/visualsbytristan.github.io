Services
Style Discovery Class
Want to learn more about style? Enroll yourself in the Style Discovery class!

In the Style Discovery, you will be presented with a crash course into style, which discusses the building blocks of authentic style and key principles (confidence, style, body types and more). This will immediately change the way you dress yourself. This service is a game-changer for anyone who is completely clueless when it comes to this or her style.

Style Breakthrough Analysis
Have no clue what your style is? This service is for you!

The Style Breakthrough Analysis is for women who seriously want to break free from their style rut! What makes this service special is we will work closely with you to analyze and develop your style. Walk away with your own Style Bible, which contains a clear picture of your style and a comprehensive guide on how to execute it. It will be a life-changing experience as this service comes with personal styling and life coaching as I dig deep through your style story.

Closet Clean Out
Do you struggle daily in figuring out what to wear for work? Do you always feel like you have nothing to wear and end up with a messy closet?

The Closet Clean Out is for people who want to explore their style, without breaking the bank! This service doesn't require any shopping whatsoever. We will teach you how to put outfits together in a way that expresses your unique style. And we will help you sort and organize your wardrobe.

Shop with Me
Take it to the next level.

In this service, I'll help you find pieces that will work well with your style, body shape and colors that will compliment your skin tone. I will also teach you some styling and shopping tips. It will be a fun shopping experience!

Christian Life Clothing

I provide 1-on-1 life coaching that are aligned with biblical teachings that unlocks freedom and wholeness. This process is not for everyone because it entails digging deep to uncovering who you are and what lies inside your heart to unlock a deeper understanding of your identity, current season and purpose.
